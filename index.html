About us

Baked bites is a small, local bakery in 18, Jalan Seri Putra 1/4, Bandar Seri Putra, 43000 Kajang, Selangor. Owned by a partnership between Nur Aireen Puteri Binti Abd Razak and Muhammad Khalid Bin Mohd Razif. We will specialize in cakes, cupcakes, cookies, donuts, danishes, breads, and custom made cakes and cupcakes. Baked Bites will be located in a convenient area and be easily accessible to all residents of the community.

Baked Bites Bakery prides itself in the exceptional attention to detail to all the cakes we will serve. We will provide to the community delicious custom made-to-order cakes and pastries for all types of events and gatherings.


Vision

To make our product was the main option in occasion’s official cake


Mision

Our company seek to provide quality, superior value and service plus with the affordable price to meet our customers’ needs and ultimate satisfaction.


Objective

To make everyone can afford a cake as one of thing that ‘must’ in every occasion with an affordable price.
